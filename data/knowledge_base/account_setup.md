# CloudDesk Account Setup & Management

## Creating Your Account

1. Go to **app.clouddesk.test/signup**.
2. Enter your name, work email, and a strong password.
3. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox.
4. Select a plan (Starter, Pro, or Business) or start a free trial.

## Resetting Your Password

If you cannot log in or have forgotten your password:
1. Click **Forgot password?** on the login page.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link (valid for 1 hour).
4. Click the link and enter a new password.
5. If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

## Updating Your Email Address

1. Log in and go to **Account → Profile**.
2. Click **Edit** next to your current email.
3. Enter the new email address and confirm with your password.
4. A verification link is sent to the new address. Click it to confirm the change.
5. Your login email updates immediately after verification.

## Updating Your Profile

- Go to **Account → Profile** to update your name, company name, or timezone.
- Profile changes save automatically.

## Enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

CloudDesk supports app-based 2FA (e.g., Google Authenticator, Authy):
1. Go to **Account → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**.
2. Click **Enable 2FA**.
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app.
4. Enter the 6-digit code from the app to confirm setup.
5. Save your backup codes in a secure location — these are shown once.

## Disabling 2FA

1. Go to **Account → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**.
2. Click **Disable 2FA** and confirm with your password.
3. If you have lost access to your authenticator app, contact support with your account email and identity verification.

## Closing Your Account

To permanently close your account, contact support at **support@clouddesk.test**. Data is deleted after 30 days per our data retention policy.
