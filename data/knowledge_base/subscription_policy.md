# CloudDesk Subscription Policy

## Plans Available

CloudDesk offers three subscription tiers: **Starter**, **Pro**, and **Business**. See `pricing.md` for current pricing.

## Billing Cycle

- All plans are billed **monthly** by default.
- Annual billing is available at a discounted rate (see pricing page).
- Your billing date is set to the date you first subscribed.

## Upgrades

- Plan upgrades take effect **immediately**.
- You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle.
- Access to upgraded features begins right away.

## Downgrades

- Plan downgrades are scheduled for the **next billing cycle**.
- You retain access to current plan features until the billing cycle ends.
- No partial refund is issued for the current cycle when downgrading.

## Cancellation

- You can cancel your subscription **at any time** from the Account → Billing settings page.
- Cancellation takes effect at the **end of the current billing period**.
- After cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days before deletion.
- You will not be charged again after the billing period ends.
- To cancel immediately and request a refund, see the Refund Policy.

## Paused or Past-Due Accounts

- If a payment fails, your account enters **past_due** status.
- CloudDesk retries the payment 3 times over 7 days.
- After 3 failed attempts, your subscription is suspended until payment is resolved.
- Suspended accounts can be reactivated by updating payment information in Account → Billing.

## Reactivation

- Cancelled accounts can be reactivated within 30 days by selecting a new plan.
- Data is restored if reactivated before the 30-day retention window expires.

## Contact

For subscription issues, open a support ticket or email **billing@clouddesk.test**.
