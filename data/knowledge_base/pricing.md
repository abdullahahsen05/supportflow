# CloudDesk Pricing

## Current Plans

### Starter — $29.99 / month
Best for individuals and small teams getting started.

**Includes:**
- Up to 3 users
- 10 GB storage
- Standard support (email, response within 1 business day)
- Core dashboard and analytics
- Monthly billing only

---

### Pro — $79.99 / month
Best for growing teams that need more power and faster support.

**Includes:**
- Up to 20 users
- 100 GB storage
- Priority support (email + chat, response within 4 hours)
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- API access
- Custom integrations
- Monthly or annual billing (annual saves 15%)

---

### Business — $199.99 / month
Best for large organizations requiring dedicated support and SLA guarantees.

**Includes:**
- Unlimited users
- 1 TB storage
- Dedicated support agent
- 4-hour SLA for critical issues
- SSO (Single Sign-On)
- Custom onboarding kit (shipped to your office)
- Advanced security and audit logs
- Annual billing only

---

## Annual Billing Discount

All plans offer a **15% discount** when billed annually instead of monthly.

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (per month) |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | $29.99 | $25.49 |
| Pro | $79.99 | $67.99 |
| Business | $199.99 | $169.99 |

---

## Add-Ons

- **Extra Storage:** $5 / 10 GB / month
- **Additional Users (Starter):** $8 / user / month beyond 3
- **Onboarding Kit (Pro):** $49.99 one-time (physical kit, shipped separately)

---

## Free Trial

New accounts receive a **14-day free trial** of the Pro plan with no credit card required. After the trial, you are prompted to select a plan or your account is moved to a read-only state.
