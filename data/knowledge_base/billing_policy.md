# CloudDesk Billing Policy

## Billing Cycle

- CloudDesk bills on a **monthly** basis, on the same date each month (your subscription start date).
- Annual billing is charged once per year at a discounted rate.
- Invoices are emailed to your registered email address and available in Account → Billing → Invoice History.

## Accepted Payment Methods

- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express).
- Payment details are stored securely via our payment processor. CloudDesk does not store raw card data.

## Failed Payments

- If a payment fails, CloudDesk will retry up to **3 times** over 7 days.
- You will be notified by email after each failed attempt.
- After 3 failed attempts, your account is placed in **past_due** status and access may be restricted.
- To resolve a failed payment, update your payment method in Account → Billing → Payment Methods.

## Duplicate Charges

- Duplicate charges are a high-priority issue for CloudDesk.
- If you see two identical charges for the same billing period on your statement:
  1. Do not dispute with your bank immediately — contact CloudDesk billing support first.
  2. Open a support ticket with category **Billing / Duplicate Charge**.
  3. Provide transaction dates, amounts, and your account email.
  4. Duplicate charges are reviewed within **2 business days** and corrected automatically.
- Billing issues that cannot be resolved automatically are escalated to a human billing agent.

## Taxes

- Prices shown are exclusive of applicable taxes.
- Tax is calculated based on your billing address.

## Billing Disputes

- For any billing concern not covered above, email **billing@clouddesk.test**.
- CloudDesk aims to resolve all billing disputes within **5 business days**.
