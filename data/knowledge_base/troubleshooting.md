# CloudDesk Troubleshooting Guide

## Login Problems

**Cannot log in / invalid password:**
1. Confirm you are using the correct email address.
2. Try resetting your password via **Forgot password?** on the login page.
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again.
4. Try an incognito / private browser window.
5. Disable browser extensions that may interfere with forms.

**Account locked:**
- After 5 failed login attempts, your account is temporarily locked for 15 minutes.
- Wait 15 minutes and try again, or reset your password.

**2FA code not working:**
- Ensure your authenticator app's time is synced (most apps have a sync option in settings).
- Use a backup code if the 6-digit code is rejected.
- Contact support if you have lost access to both your authenticator and backup codes.

## Dashboard Loading Issues

**Dashboard shows blank screen or loads slowly:**
1. Hard-refresh the page: `Ctrl + Shift + R` (Windows) or `Cmd + Shift + R` (Mac).
2. Clear browser cache: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data.
3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
4. Check **status.clouddesk.test** for any ongoing incidents.
5. Disable browser extensions, especially ad blockers or script blockers.

**Data not updating:**
- Wait 30 seconds and refresh. Real-time data can have a brief sync delay.
- Log out and log back in to force a session refresh.

## Notification Issues

**Not receiving email notifications:**
1. Check your spam / junk folder.
2. Add **no-reply@clouddesk.test** to your safe senders list.
3. Go to **Account → Notifications** and confirm your preferences are enabled.
4. Verify that your registered email address is correct under Account → Profile.

**Too many notifications:**
- Adjust notification frequency in **Account → Notifications → Preferences**.

## Still Having Issues?

If none of the above steps resolve your issue, open a support ticket with:
- Your account email
- A description of the problem
- Browser and operating system version
- Any error messages you see

Support responds within **1 business day** for standard plans and within **4 hours** for Business plan customers.
