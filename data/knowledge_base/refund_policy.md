# CloudDesk Refund Policy

## Overview

CloudDesk is committed to fair and transparent billing. This document outlines when and how refunds are issued.

## Eligibility

- Refund requests must be submitted within **14 days** of the original charge.
- Physical products (onboarding kits, accessories) are eligible for a full refund if returned in original condition within 14 days of delivery.
- Subscription charges are refundable within 14 days of the initial purchase or renewal, provided the account has not been used beyond normal evaluation limits.
- Charges older than 14 days are generally non-refundable but may be reviewed at CloudDesk's discretion.

## Duplicate Charges

If you believe you have been charged more than once for the same product or billing period:
- Contact CloudDesk billing support immediately.
- Provide your account email, the dates of both charges, and any transaction references.
- Duplicate charges are reviewed within **2 business days** and refunded automatically if confirmed.
- Do not dispute the charge with your bank before contacting us — this slows down the resolution process.

## Subscription Refunds

- Monthly subscription charges are refundable within 14 days of charge.
- Annual subscription refunds are prorated for unused months if cancelled within 30 days.
- Charges for upgrades are refundable within 14 days if the upgrade was made in error.
- Downgrade credits are applied to future billing cycles, not issued as cash refunds.

## How to Request a Refund

1. Email **billing@clouddesk.test** with subject: "Refund Request — [your account email]"
2. Include the charge date, amount, and reason.
3. Alternatively, open a support ticket and select category: **Billing / Refund**.

## Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** and returned to the original payment method.
