# CloudDesk Shipping Policy

## What We Ship

CloudDesk is primarily a software company, but we ship physical **Onboarding Kits** and optional hardware accessories to new Business plan customers or as add-on purchases.

## Processing Time

- Orders are processed within **1–2 business days** of payment confirmation.
- Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed the next business day.

## Shipping Time

- Standard shipping: **3–5 business days** after dispatch.
- Expedited shipping: **1–2 business days** after dispatch (available at checkout for an additional fee).
- International shipping: **7–14 business days** depending on destination.

## Tracking

- A tracking number is assigned and emailed to you when your order is dispatched.
- Tracking numbers follow the format **TRK-CD-XXXX**.
- You can check your order status and tracking number in the **Orders** section of your account dashboard.
- If you have a tracking number but the carrier website shows no updates, allow 24 hours for the carrier to process the shipment.

## Shipping Regions

- CloudDesk ships domestically and to most international destinations.
- Some accessories may not be available for international shipment due to customs restrictions.

## Lost or Damaged Shipments

- If your order has not arrived within 10 business days of the estimated delivery date, contact support.
- Damaged items must be reported within **5 days of delivery** with photographic evidence.
- CloudDesk will replace lost or damaged items at no additional cost after investigation.

## Returns

- Physical items can be returned within 14 days of delivery. See the **Refund Policy** for details.
- Return shipping costs are covered by CloudDesk for defective or incorrectly shipped items.

## Contact

For shipping inquiries, open a support ticket or email **support@clouddesk.test**.
